Utah

I consider myself to be pretty lucky! O live in the prettiest state!

I've lived in Utah for 20 years and there's still lots to explore. Three hours away from the city and the landscape "wows".

Every time friends, or family visit Utah I take them to these dramatic scenes.

You never saw a place as beautiful as Utah!

I camp-out a lot. I never stay in campgrounds. Just pull off the road and you rarely have company nearby

The dirt roads go on for miles and miles in Utah. Camping abounds

It doesn't matter the season. Our Southern Utah desert is never that cold

I think its time to pack my bags and plan an excursion
